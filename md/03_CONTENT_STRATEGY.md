# 03 — Content Strategy

## Content goal

The content must reposition Gurtej from “student developer” to “creative developer building premium digital experiences.”

Do not sound like a resume.

Do not over-explain.

Use short, sharp, confident copy.

## Primary headline options

Use one of these:

### Option A

> Digital experiences with presence.

### Option B

> Building premium interfaces with motion, clarity, and cinematic direction.

### Option C

> Creative developer crafting visual systems for ambitious brands.

### Option D

> Interfaces that feel deliberate before they feel technical.

Recommended hero combination:

**Name:**  
> GURTEJ

**Subheading:**  
> Creative developer crafting premium digital experiences through motion, visual systems, and sharp execution.

## Writing rules

Use:

- short sentences
- strong nouns
- active voice
- no fake awards
- no empty adjectives like “passionate” unless backed by proof
- no long paragraphs
- no “I am a hardworking student”
- no “I have knowledge of HTML, CSS, JS”

## Words to use

- presence
- cinematic
- systems
- premium
- deliberate
- clarity
- motion
- structure
- restraint
- interface
- experience
- conversion
- storytelling

## Words to avoid

- passionate
- hardworking
- enthusiastic
- basic
- beginner
- learner
- awesome
- modern
- beautiful
- amazing

These words are too weak or too common.
